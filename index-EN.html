Welcome !

I am a PhD student of statistics, involved in environmental and agricultural applications at INRA, University of Bordeaux, attached with INRIA. My supervisors are Anne Gégout-Petit and Lucia Guérin-Dubrana.

Ever since my Master 2 internship, I have always worked on a variety of ecological and agricultural problems, such as plant disease modelling, with both biologists and statisticians. I am particularly interested in:

  • Point Pattern Analysis
  • Geostatistical methods
  • Spatio-temporal modelling
    • Bayesian hierarchical models
    • Autologistic model
  • Time series

And have been able to develop all these aspects throughout my on-going research.
